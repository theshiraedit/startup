Where Are You in Your Startup Journey?

💡 Step 1: Research & Validate Your Idea

Start by understanding your market. Use these tools:

  • 🔍 Google Trends - Check if people are searching for your idea.
  • 📊 SurveyMonkey - Gather insights from potential users.
  • 🎯 Product Hunt - See if similar ideas exist and how they perform.

✅ Once you see demand, move on to prototyping!

📈 Step 2: Validate Your Idea

Before building a product, make sure people will buy it.

  • 🛠 Carrd - Build a simple landing page for your idea.
  • 💰 Stripe - Set up a fake "preorder" button to test if people are willing to pay.
  • 📢 Google Ads - Run small ads to test market demand.

✅ If people sign up or show interest, you're on the right track!

💰 Step 3: Get Funding

Explore different ways to get funding:

  • 👥 AngelList - Connect with startup investors.
  • 🏦 SBA Loans - Apply for small business loans.
  • 🚀 Y Combinator - Apply to top accelerator programs.

✅ Once funded, focus on building your MVP.

🚀 Step 4: Scale Your Business

Growth strategies for taking your business to the next level:

  • 🔄 Zapier - Automate business operations.
  • 📢 Facebook Ads - Scale customer acquisition.
  • 🌎 LinkedIn - Build strategic partnerships.

✅ Once you have traction, consider international expansion.
